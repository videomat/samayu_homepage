Carlos

Dharma Coach

Carlos is a certified Dharma Coach with over 10 years of experience helping individuals discover their life's purpose and live in alignment with their values. He has a background in psychology and mindfulness, and his approach is grounded in compassion, empathy, and practical guidance. Carlos's mission is to empower others to create a life of meaning, fulfillment, and joy.

Carlos's journey began with a deep curiosity about the human experience and a desire to understand the nature of suffering and happiness. He spent years studying various spiritual traditions, including Buddhism, Hinduism, and Taoism, and practicing meditation and mindfulness. Through his own personal transformation, Carlos realized the power of Dharma coaching to help others navigate life's challenges and find their true path. He is committed to providing a safe and supportive space for clients to explore their inner wisdom and create lasting positive change.
